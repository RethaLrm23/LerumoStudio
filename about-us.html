About Us

Welcome to Lerumo Studio

We are a freelance photography and videography business with over 10 years of experience capturing precious moments. Our journey started with a passion for visual storytelling and a desire to preserve memories. Over the years, we have honed our skills and expanded our services to cater to both corporate and family functions.

At Lerumo Studio, we specialize in photo and video capturing, editing, packaging, and framing. We understand the importance of preserving your special moments in the most beautiful and meaningful way possible. In addition to our core services, we also offer video and photographic restoration. If you have old VHS cassettes or photographs that you'd like to convert to digital media, we can help bring them back to life.

Our goal is to provide exceptional quality and customer satisfaction. We strive to create visual masterpieces that tell your unique story. Whether it's a corporate event, a wedding, or a family gathering, we will capture every moment with precision and creativity.
